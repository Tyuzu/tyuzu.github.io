CLC's Elkie has partnered with a law firm and sent a legal letter to CUBE Entertainment. The letter includes the allegations of ignoring the group and not paying her for her acting in dramas. 
Due to company's management and structural changes, CUBE Entertainment clearly informed CLC members that it would no longer support CLC's group development in 2020. Since then, CUBE Entertainment also did not provide reasonable planning for Elkie's entertainment business'.

CUBE has not yet responded.
